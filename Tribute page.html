Rohit Sharma Tribute Page

Biography

Rohit Sharma, nicknamed "Hitman," is a renowned Indian cricketer known for his exceptional batting skills. He holds several records, including the highest individual score in an ODI match (264 runs) and the most double centuries in ODI cricket. As the captain of the Indian cricket team in Test and ODI formats, he has led India to numerous victories and is celebrated as one of the greatest openers in cricket history

Achievements

  • Highest Individual Score in ODI: Rohit Sharma holds the record for the highest individual score in an ODI match, scoring an unbeaten 264 runs against Sri Lanka in 2014
  • Most Double Centuries in ODI: He is the only player in cricket history to have scored three double centuries in ODI cricket
  • Five Centuries in a Single World Cup: During the 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup, Rohit became the only batsman to score five centuries in a single edition of the tournament
